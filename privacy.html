Privacy Policy

Last updated:

1. Who we are

SwiftCargo Transport ("SwiftCargo", "we", "us") provides domestic and EU cross‑border cargo transportation services.

2. Data we collect

  • Contact details: name, email, phone.
  • Shipment details: pickup/delivery addresses, package dimensions and weight, service preferences.
  • Billing info: payment confirmations from our provider (we do not store full card numbers).
  • Usage data: device, browser, pages visited, and interactions for security and analytics.

3. How we use data

  • Provide and manage shipments, pickups, routing, and delivery updates.
  • Process payments and prevent fraud.
  • Offer support and respond to inquiries.
  • Improve our services and website reliability.

4. Legal bases

We process personal data based on contract performance, legitimate interests (service operation, security), legal obligations, and consent where required.

5. Sharing

We share necessary data with carriers, payment processors, customer support tools, and analytics providers. We do not sell personal data.

6. International transfers

When data is transferred outside the EEA, we rely on appropriate safeguards (e.g., standard contractual clauses).

7. Retention

We keep data as long as needed for the purposes above and to meet legal obligations, then delete or anonymize it.

8. Your rights

Subject to law, you may request access, correction, deletion, or portability of your data, and object to or restrict certain processing. You may also withdraw consent where processing is based on consent.

9. Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect personal data. No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure.

10. Cookies

We may use cookies or similar technologies for essential site functions and analytics. You can control cookies via your browser settings.

11. Contact

For privacy questions or requests, email privacy@swiftcargotransport.eu.
